What is the molality?
The molality is a measurement of the mumber of the solute moles in a solution that corresponds to 1kg of solvent. The molality is measured by mol/kg.
How to calculate molality?
To calculate molality you should use the following formula:
m = mol / kg
where:
m = molality
mol = moles of solute
kg = kilogram of solvent
Why use molality?
The molality is used to study the properties of solutions related to vapor pressure and changes in temperature because the value of molality does not change with changes in temperature.
